About us

How Women's Football is born

It all started when two friends 🤝 attended a Women's Champions League match between Juventus and Bayern. 🏟️ To our surprise, the stadium was almost empty, with only about 1,000 spectators. We were impressed by how accessible the experience was: an atmosphere full of passion and dedication, but lacking the audience it deserved. 💜 The next day, an idea was born: to create a platform that would highlight women's football, giving it the attention and visibility it has long deserved. ⚽✨ And so, Women's Football was born — a project driven by our shared love for the game and a commitment to making a difference. 🚀

The Vision

Our goal is to become the go-to source for fans 🏆, offering everything they need to stay informed, inspired, and engaged with the world of women's football. From live stats 📊 to player profiles, our platform is designed to celebrate the athletes, teams, and moments that define the sport. 🥇 And as we grow, we'll continue to innovate, ensuring fans have access to the best content and tools to follow the game. 🌟
